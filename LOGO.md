## Usage

The logo uses:
- **Primary Color**: #000000 (Black) - Background
- **Accent Color**: #FF6B35 (Rust Orange) - Text and accents

## Brand Guidelines

- Use the Rust orange (#FF6B35) as the primary accent color
- Maintain the circular design for consistency
- The "RIT" text should always be clearly readable
